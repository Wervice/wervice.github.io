Whitespace

Repairing
Wervice Whitespace is an online emulator for a modern, clean, and fast* desktop environment. It has helpful and free pre-installed applications for business and home. Thanks to common web APIs and web technologies, it works secure in the browser, and you can store your own files and information on your device and not in a third-party cloud.

Interline Word

Interline Word is a free text editor on the web for simple text-only files. The interface is of modern and clean design. It has a built in storage to save files on your device. You can edit, open and download your files. It is helpful when writing long mails, shopping lists or a fast* note.

Open Interline Word

LockScreenCrypter

Wervice LockScreenCrypter is a web app to encrypt your private or sensible messages on your device. For example, you want to write somebody secret information, but you are worried, somebody else could also read it, for example on your recipient's lock screen, you can encrypt the message, and your recipient has to decrypt it first. But don't forget, that your recipient also needs to know this tool, and that's not a replacement for other important measures.

NoteDash

NoteDash is a simple tool to organize notes on a virtual wall. You can write, edit and delete notes and sort on the dashboard with a color. And there is a nice dark mode for night owls.
